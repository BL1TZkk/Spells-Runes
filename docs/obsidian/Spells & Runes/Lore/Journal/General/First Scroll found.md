Found something today. Inside a chest; underneath all the junk — scrap metal, tools, broken glass etc. It wasn't trash.  
A scroll. Ancient. Faded at the ends (beginning & end) so it's bigger than my hands. But what's left is readable. Read it twice.  

I don't completely get it. But that isn't why I'm excited. I'm excited because parts of it seemed familiar — as if reading a story describing something you've done or seen before but could never put into words. This person obviously knew they were talking about something real. I think I'm sure about that now.  

I'll be saving it. I have begun documenting items such as scrolls, writings, articles etc. that seem to connect with events happening to me after the ritual. I haven't figured out what I am doing yet. Perhaps nothing. Possibly something that makes sense once I gather up enough pieces.  

I believe there are many other items just like this one.
