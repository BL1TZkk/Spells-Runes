It burns. That's the first thing I've noticed, and will notice for what feels far too long.  
Not the skin (that is perfectly fine). What lies beneath. Someone has gone ahead and lit something that has been burning for a very long time.  
The paste was applied cold. This really caught me off guard. The heat from whatever it does to this stuff when you apply it doesn't seem to appear. When you do apply it, it appears as though it hasn't changed; yet, roughly thirty seconds after applying it, all of the sudden, it changes!  

Heat began in the chest. More "present" than "painful". Like being too close to a fire -- but the fire is inside of me and radiating outward into my arms, hands, and face. So, I got up to sit down because standing seemed to require an awful lot more control than I could muster at that moment.  

What was truly weird was the anger. Not at anyone. Just pure anger. Available to me like a new tool I hadn't realized I possessed. But I didn't use it. I simply sat there breathing while letting it settle. Eventually, it settled. Almost completely.  
I feel like something shifted in the wrong direction and then corrected itself and is now sitting slightly off center. Still good. Simply different than it was previously. A little warmer. A little louder on the inside.  

I'm going to stay here until I can once again rely on my hands.
